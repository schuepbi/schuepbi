### About

I operate at the intersection of information security, digitalisation, and internal product development.

As Head of Digitalisation, I am responsible for designing and implementing internal systems that support core business functions — including administration, HR, and executive operations. My focus is on building reliable, maintainable tools that improve process efficiency, transparency, and decision-making.

In parallel, I contribute to the development and operation of our Information Security Management System (ISMS), translating policies, risks, controls, and evidence into auditable, operational processes in line with ISO 27001. This includes close collaboration with stakeholders across the organisation to ensure security is effectively embedded beyond IT.

In addition, I am developing an independent product initiative.

From a technical perspective, I primarily work with Python for applications, automation, and data-driven tools, and Rust for performance-critical services and CLI applications. My workflow is centred on macOS and terminal-based tooling, leveraging AI-assisted development to accelerate iteration — with strict human oversight for all security-relevant changes.

### Current Focus

- **ISMS:** Strengthening governance through clear control ownership, structured documentation, and effective risk treatment.
- **Digitalisation:** Designing and delivering internal systems that streamline workflows across business functions.
- **Product Development:** Advancing an independent product initiative alongside my core responsibilities.
